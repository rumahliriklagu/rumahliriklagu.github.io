Terlihat jelas pada lirik, setelah mereka tidak bunuh diri, mereka menghadapi semua masalah tersebut dan melarikan diri. Meskipun ayahnya menentangnya, mereka terus mempertahankan keyakinan cintanya tersebut sampai akhirnya direstui. Merekapun akhirnya menikah, menjadi raja dan ratu dan hidup bahagia selamanya. BAHASA INGGRIS:

"Love Story" tells the story of Romeo and Juliet. Unlike the original story which tends to be full of drama and tragic ending because the couple committed suicide, the story of this song seems to end happily. Where songwriters seem to want to improve the story of Romeo and Juliet by writing down what will happen if they don't commit suicide. Seen clearly in the lyrics, after they did not commit suicide, they faced all these problems and ran away. Even though his father opposed him, they continued to maintain his love of belief until finally being blessed. They eventually married, became kings and queens and lived happily ever after. The lesson we can learn from this song is that what becomes our belief, as long as it is all true, then fight for it. Because love is not because of coercion, because love is a beautiful thing that should be the happiest story, not even end tragically.
