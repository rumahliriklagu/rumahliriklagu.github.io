40. please jawab yg a,b,c,d . lagu Rihanna - diamonds


Answer: 

Here's the possible answers for a,b and c, but not for d, as the instruction is to sing, which is very unlikely to be written here. 
a. 1. the most possible word which has the closest meaning to that phrase in the column is 'vision' though in this song, such has no related meaning to foresee a future.
    2. The word 'line of light' in the lyric which has the same meaning is  'sun rays'.
    3. The word 'great joy and emotion' has the same meaning as 'ecstasy' in the lyric.

b. The figurative language from the sentence 'We're like diamonds in the sky.' is a metaphor. 
c. The message of the song above is more like an expression of a couple that is in the peak of their love and they feel so special with it. When people are in love, they tend to be like the main attraction of the world as the world feels like on their feet for them only.
