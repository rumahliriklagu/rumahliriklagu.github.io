20. contoh describing tentang my best friend


Describing my best friend:

She was tall, brown skin, curly tied up hair. Her smile is charming with pure white teeth. Her name is Susan. She's my best friend. We've been friends since elementary school. Our houses are close together so we always play together every day. Susan's house is very close to mine, only a few houses apart.

Susan loves to draw. Her hands are very skilled in drawing anything. Susan can draw landscapes, draw people, draw animals, and she can even draw me.

Susan has a very cute little brother, he is four years old. We always play together. Susan loves and cares for her brother very much.

Susan is also very fond of books. She has a lot of books in her room. There are comic books, short story books, novels, and other science books. Susan always showed me the new book she got from her father and told me about its contents.

Susan really likes meatballs. I also like meatballs. On holidays we always eat meatballs together at our favorite restaurant. Susan loves spicy meatballs and eats a lot of them.

Pembahasan

Describing someone adalah kamu menceritakan dan mendeskripsikan seseorang, misalnya temanmu. Kamu ceritakan aspek fisik, nama, hobinya apa, rumahnya dimana, sejak kapan kalian bersahabat, dan juga keluarganya.

Kamu bisa memakai cara ini: kamu tulis dulu deskripsi tersebut dalam bahasa Indonesia lalu kamu terjemahkan ke dalam bahasa Inggris dengan bantuan kamus bahasa Inggris. Dengan banyak menulis maka nantinya kamu akan mudah membuat tulisan langsung dalam bahasa Inggris.
