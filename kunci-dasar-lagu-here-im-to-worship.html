29. im sleppy. i (keep,not) .............. my eyes open. i (go)....... to bed before i fall asleep right here


Answer:

The sentences above are cause and effect kind or has such relation in them as there are both included in there. The tense is in present and possibly in future at the end as it may show a spontaneous decision.
If they are broken down into parts, 'I'm sleepy' is the cause and in present.
The rest of the sentences are effect and prediction, the effect is 'I.........my eyes open.' and the solution is 'I ..... go to bed before I fall asleep right here.'

So, here's the answer:
I'm sleepy. I can't keep my eyes open. I'll just go to be before I fall asleep here.

Kelas: SMA
Mapel: English
Kategori: grammar reviewkelas: SMA
Materi: modal auxiliaries
kata kunci: should, would, modal

2. Besaran Turunan

Besaran turunan adalah besaran yang satuannya diturunkan dari besaran pokok.

Contoh :

- kecepatan = perpindahan dibagi waktu

Yang berarti satuan luas diturunkan dari besaran pokok panjang (perpindahan) dan waktu.

Sehingga satuan kecepatan dalam SI adalah m/s

- luas = panjang x lebar = m x m = m2

- volume = panjang x lebar x tinggi = m x m x m = m3

- massa jenis = massa/volume = kg/m3
