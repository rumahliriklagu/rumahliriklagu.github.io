1. The boys are.... than the girls​


Jawaban:

Degrees of Comparison:

The boys are more attractive than the girls​.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran Degrees of Comparison (derajat perbandingan) dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Kita menambahkan akhiran "-er" dan "than" pada kata sifat satu suku kata dan awalan "more" dan "than" pada kata sifat lebih dari dua suku kata untuk membentuk Comparative Degree (gelar perbandingan), contohnya:

Bigger than = lebih besar daripada (satu suku kata dari "Big")

More Beautiful than = lebih Cantik dari pada (empat suku kata "Beautiful")

More Attractive than = lebih Menarik dari pada (tiga suku kata "Attractive")

6. The ratio of boys to girls in a competition is 12 : 17. The difference number of boys and girls students is 30. How many boys are there?


Jawab:

There are 72 boys in the competition.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

The ratio of boys to girls = 12 : 17

The difference number of them = 30

Q: How many boys are there?

METHOD 1

Let the difference number of boys and girls be another "object" in comparation.

Then, we can redefine the ratio of boys (B) to girls (G) to the difference of them (D) as follows:

B : G : D = 12 : 17 : (17-12)

B : G : D = 12 : 17 : 5

This way we can get:

B = (12/5) x 30

   = 12 x 6

   = 72

CONCLUSION:

∴ There are 72 boys in the competition.

METHOD 2

Let the number of boys taken by B, and the number of girls taken by G.

Based on the ratio, we can get:

G = (17/12)B

The difference number of them is 30, so this is how we calculate B:

G - B = 30

(17/12)B - B = 30

(17/12)B - (12/12)B = 30

(5/12)B = 30

B = 30 : (5/12)

   = 30 × (12/5)

   = 6 × 12

   = 72

CONCLUSION:

∴ There are 72 boys in the competition.
