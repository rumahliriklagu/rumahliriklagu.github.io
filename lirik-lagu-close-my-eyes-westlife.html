5. apa fungsi sosial, theme, verse dan chorus lagu westlife - my love ?


Answer: 

The song 'My Love' by Westlife has rather deeper meaning than just romance. If you look carefully at the lyrics, therein lies a profound or wider meaning of love here. 
The social function of the song is to express your longing (or in this case the band's longing) to their homeland, Ireland, where their family lives. The love in this song is the love for their origin, place of birth or family back home. 
The theme of the song is the person's longing for their country of origin or home (can be homeland), where they have left for a while. 
The verse of the song tells how one feels emptiness inside and remembers the days when they were still with the one they left behind at home. Such can be concluded from the phrase 'a hole inside my heart', 'reaching for the love that seems so far', 'the days we had, the songs we sang together' . 
The chorus of the song indicates that one hopes and pray that one can be together again with their family after they have been away from home for long, as it is said 'hope my dreams will take me there, where the skies are blue to see you once again.'   

Kelas: SMA
Mapel: English
Kategori: song intrepretation

8. apa pesan moral dari lagu Westlife-my love. Kalau bisa pakai bahasa Inggris


In the music video, it begins in an airport, then Westlife's flight is cancelled and Bryan says he's going home. The others follow him, and they go through a little town, and end up singing the last part on a clifftop overlooking the ocean. 
I always thought it was more about loving a place rather than a person, from the way they sing "over seas from coast to coast, to find the place I love the most, where the skies are blue" - it's like a song for their home country. 
Or maybe it's just about having a true love overseas and wanting to get back to her. Songs can have different meanings for everyone!

35. makna lagu "home" westlife​


Jawaban:

Makna Lagu "Home" (David Foster ft. Micheal Buble)

Lagu ini sudah sangat lama, namun karena lagu ini sangat indah, maka sampai sekarang lagu ini masih ngehits. Makna yang sangat mendalam, romantis namun tidak cengeng, serta nada-nada yang indah secara harmonisasi dan musik yang easylistening merupakan gambaran yang sesuai dengan lagu ini.

Secara garis besar, lagu ini mengisahkan tentang seorang pasangan yang berpisah karena sang lelaki ingin mengejar cita-cita-nya.
